Passepartout

A non-official, user-friendly OpenVPN® client for iOS. Soon for macOS.

  • Presets for multiple providers (virtually any)
  • Trusted networks
  • Siri shortcuts
  • Override network settings
  • No background activities
  • Wise on battery
